Option A

Ischia: island, beaches & hot springs

Base yourselves in Forio, the budget-friendly side of the island with sunsets and easy access to Citara. This is the most “we are on holiday” option: beach mornings, romantic village views, a water-taxi feeling day, and a memorable hot-spring finale.

Budget target per person

3 nights room share in Forio€150–€210
Naples ⇄ Ischia ferries€35–€50
Island buses / limited taxis€25–€45
Food, gelato, simple drinks€120–€150
Beaches / extras€20–€45
Optional Poseidon thermal park+€45–€50
Total€430–€500
Keep it under €500 by choosing either Poseidon OR a nicer dinner, not both. Flights are not included because they change a lot.
1Arrival

Naples → ferry to Ischia → Forio sunset swim

Arrive in Naples, go to the port, take the ferry or hydrofoil to Ischia, then head to Forio. If there is time, do an easy swim at Citara Beach or Chiaia, then dinner by the sea.

SunsetLow stressFirst swim
2Beach

Citara Beach + optional Poseidon Thermal Gardens

A comfortable beach day with proper swimming and lidos. Add Poseidon if you want a “treat ourselves” moment with thermal pools and sea access, or skip it to keep the trip cheaper.

CitaraThermal poolsRelaxed
3Best day

Sant’Angelo + Maronti Beach

Start with the pretty pedestrian-feeling village of Sant’Angelo, then spend the hot part of the day at Maronti Beach. Take a water taxi if it is running to make the day feel extra special.

Romantic villageBig beachWater taxi
4Finale

Sorgeto hot springs or final Citara swim

Choose Sorgeto for a unique rocky hot-spring swim by the sea, or keep the final morning easy with one last beach swim near Forio before the ferry back.

SorgetoFinal swimEasy exit
Option B

East Sicily: Ortigia, sea caves & beach day

Base yourselves in Ortigia / Syracuse. This is the easier-budget option: romantic evenings, a boat trip around sea caves and swim spots, then a sandy beach day at Fontane Bianche or Arenella.

Budget target per person

3 nights room share in Syracuse€135–€195
Catania airport ⇄ Syracuse€12–€35
Ortigia sea-caves boat tour€25–€40
Beach transfer / buses / taxi split€25–€50
Food, gelato, simple drinks€120–€150
Beach extras / contingency€30–€50
Total€350–€470
This leaves more cushion for flights or one nicer dinner. For the budget, stay just outside Ortigia if island rooms are expensive.
1Arrival

Catania → Ortigia + seaside evening

Fly into Catania, transfer to Syracuse, check in, then wander Ortigia. Swim from the rocky platforms if you arrive early, then do sunset, dinner, and gelato.

OrtigiaWalkableRomantic dinner
2Boat

Sea caves + Plemmirio-style swim stops

Book a morning or sunset boat tour for Ortigia views, sea caves, coastal swimming, and a more adventurous feeling without needing a car.

Boat tourSea cavesSwim stops
3Beach

Fontane Bianche or Arenella

Your proper sandy beach day. Go early, keep it simple, swim, chill, and return to Ortigia for a pretty final-night dinner.

Sandy beachClear waterEasy day
4Finale

Final swim or Aci Trezza before airport

If the flight is early, stay simple with breakfast and a last Ortigia sea view. If the flight is later, add Aci Trezza / Aci Castello for volcanic-rock coastline near Catania.
